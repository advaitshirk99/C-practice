Queues:
1) Queues are linear data structures that are open at both ends and the operations are performed in first in first out (FIFO) order.
2) One end is used to insert data (enqueue) while the other end is used to remove data (dequeue).

Applications of queues:
1) Used in CPU scheduling algorithm FCFS (first come first serve)
2) Applied on OS to handle the interruptions
3) Buffer for devices like keyboards
